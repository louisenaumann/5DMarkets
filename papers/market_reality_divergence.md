# Market Reality Divergence Principle

Financial markets do not directly represent objective world state.

Markets continuously attempt to compute the economic consequences of changing real world conditions.

However, market computation may temporarily diverge from objective world reality.

------------------------------------------------------------
WORLD STATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Define:

W(t)

where

W(t)

represents objective external world conditions.

Examples include:

Regulatory changes

Supply chain conditions

Demographic shifts

Disease prevalence

Manufacturing capacity

Interest rate environment

Consumer demand changes

Patent expiration schedules

------------------------------------------------------------
MARKET COMPUTATIONAL STATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Define:

M(t)

where

M(t)

represents market interpretation of world state.

This includes:

Institutional expectations

Algorithmic positioning

Investor sentiment

Capital allocation decisions

Market belief systems

------------------------------------------------------------
MARKET REALITY DIVERGENCE
------------------------------------------------------------

Define divergence:

Δ_i(t)

=

|W_i(t) - M_i(t)|

where

Δ_i(t)

measures divergence between objective world conditions and market interpretation.

------------------------------------------------------------
CENTRAL PREDICTION
------------------------------------------------------------

Large divergence predicts future capital correction.

When markets miscompute objective world conditions, capital allocation eventually adjusts.

Future capital migration tends to reduce divergence over time.

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
------------------------------------------------------------

The market is continuously attempting to compute reality.

Temporary mismatch between objective world conditions and market interpretation creates predictive opportunity.

The objective of 5DMarkets is to identify divergence before market correction occurs.
