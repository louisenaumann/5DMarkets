# Fundamental Market Evolution Equation

The 5DMarkets framework proposes that financial markets continuously evolve through distributed computation attempting to reduce divergence between objective world state and market interpreted state.

We define the governing equation of market evolution as:

dM(t)/dt

=

-∇Δ(t)

+

C(t)

+

H(t)

+

E(t)

-

μM(t)

------------------------------------------------------------
TERM 1
------------------------------------------------------------

-∇Δ(t)

Reality correction pressure.

The market continuously attempts to reduce divergence between world state and market interpretation.

------------------------------------------------------------
TERM 2
------------------------------------------------------------

C(t)

Distributed market computation.

Represents collective information processing performed by institutions, algorithms, and market participants.

------------------------------------------------------------
TERM 3
------------------------------------------------------------

H(t)

Path dependent hysteresis memory.

Historical trajectory constrains future evolution.

------------------------------------------------------------
TERM 4
------------------------------------------------------------

E(t)

Exogenous forcing state.

Unexpected external events entering the system.

------------------------------------------------------------
TERM 5
------------------------------------------------------------

μM(t)

System friction.

Represents barriers preventing immediate market correction.

Examples include transaction costs, liquidity constraints, behavioral bias, and institutional inertia.

------------------------------------------------------------
INTERPRETATION
------------------------------------------------------------

The market continuously attempts to compute objective world reality.

When market interpretation diverges from world state, correction pressure emerges.

Distributed computation, memory effects, external forcing, and system friction jointly determine future market evolution.
