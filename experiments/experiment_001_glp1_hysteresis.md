# Experiment 001 — GLP1 Market Hysteresis

Objective

Determine whether identical present pharmaceutical equity prices produce different future return distributions depending on adoption trajectory history.

------------------------------------------------------------

Hypothesis

Two pharmaceutical equities occupying identical present valuation states may possess different future return probabilities depending on historical adoption path.

------------------------------------------------------------

Case Study Assets

LLY

NVO

------------------------------------------------------------

Theoretical Motivation

Drug adoption follows biological growth systems.

Prescription growth frequently demonstrates nonlinear expansion.

A company experiencing accelerating physician adoption occupies a different latent market state than a company experiencing decelerating adoption.

Even if current price remains identical.

------------------------------------------------------------

Expected Conclusion

Current price is insufficient for prediction.

Historical adoption trajectory influences future state.

This supports hysteresis operator H(S_t).
