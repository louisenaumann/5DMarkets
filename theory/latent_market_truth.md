# Latent Market Truth

Financial markets possess an underlying hidden state independent of any predictive model.

We define this as Latent Market Truth.

------------------------------------------------------------
TRUE MARKET STATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Define:

Ω*(t)

where

Ω*(t)

represents the true hidden market state.

This state exists independently of observation.

Components may include:

True capital allocation distribution

True institutional intent

True algorithmic positioning

True liquidity conditions

True market attractiveness potential

------------------------------------------------------------
ESTIMATED MARKET STATE
------------------------------------------------------------

The 5DMarkets framework produces an estimated market state.

Define:

Ω^(t)

where

Ω^(t)

represents model estimated hidden market state.

The framework does not define market reality.

The framework estimates hidden market reality.

------------------------------------------------------------
ATTRACTIVENESS ESTIMATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Each asset possesses true attractiveness potential.

Define:

U*_i(t)

where

U*_i(t)

represents true market attractiveness.

The framework estimates:

Û_i(t)

where

Û_i(t)

approximates true attractiveness.

------------------------------------------------------------
OBJECTIVE
------------------------------------------------------------

The objective of 5DMarkets is not direct prediction.

The objective is estimation of hidden market truth.

Formally:

min | Ω^(t) - Ω*(t) |

The framework seeks to minimize estimation error between observed market information and true hidden market state.

------------------------------------------------------------
CORE PRINCIPLE
------------------------------------------------------------

The market exists independently of any predictive model.

5DMarkets does not define reality.

5DMarkets estimates hidden market reality as accurately as possible.
